§ 01   Executive Summary

The proposal, in a single page

An AI-native operating layer between donors and every nonprofit.

Philanthropy OS is built around six AI surfaces — three donor-facing, three backend. Profiling agents understand intent. A monthly giving agent automates allocation. LLM ingestion + web grounding builds profiles for charities not yet on the platform. Crowd-sourced public sentiment from Reddit and X augments the Trust Index. Together they turn passive intent into automated, high-signal giving.

SELF-LEARNING PLATFORM Every AI surface tracks its own performance against the industry benchmarks laid out in slide 3 — conversion lift, catalog expansion, sentiment freshness, match capture, crisis latency, and planning adoption. The platform optimizes itself to beat these standards, continuously.

§ 02   The AI ROI Gap

Why these six AI surfaces — and why now

The legacy stack can't profile, ingest, or act in real time. AI can.

Each gap below maps to a specific surface from the previous slide — measurable today, closeable only by an AI-native architecture. This is the ROI envelope a Gates grant unlocks.

GAP 01 → AGENT 01 · PROFILING
2%
Donor-page conversion on charity sites today.

Static donate buttons can't read intent. A live profiling agent that watches dwell, clicks, and follow-through lifts conversion 3–5× by surfacing the right ask at the right moment.

UNLOCK · profiling agent → ~3× lift on the same traffic
GAP 02 → AGENT 04 · INGESTION
<3%
Of 1.8M US 501(c)(3)s sit in any donor platform's catalog.

The other 97% never enroll. LLM ingestion grounded in web search builds a live profile for every charity — not just the ones who self-onboard. The long tail, indexed for the first time.

UNLOCK · LLM ingestion → ~30× catalog expansion
GAP 03 → AGENT 05 · SENTIMENT
12–18mo
Lag on Charity Navigator and IRS-derived ratings.

By the time a 990 is parsed, the controversy is over. AI sentiment from Reddit, X, and forums adds a fifth, real-time trust signal — controversies surface in days, not fiscal years.

UNLOCK · 5-source Trust Index → ~365× faster signal
GAP 04 → AGENT 02 · MONTHLY GIVING
$7B
Employer match left unclaimed annually.

Recurring "set-and-forget" donations don't leverage employee matching. An autonomous monthly agent rebalances against live signal and surfaces match at execution time.

UNLOCK · monthly agent → ~2× effective dollar via match + leverage
GAP 05 → AGENT 06 · CROWD CAMPAIGNS
72hr
Lag from breaking crisis to vetted, fundable responder.

By the time donors find a trusted responder, attention has moved on. AI scouts validate emerging fundraisers in minutes — Crisis Responder Mode auto-allocates a donor's pre-approved emergency budget.

UNLOCK · Crisis Pulse → minutes, not days
GAP 06 → AGENT 03 · PLANNING COPILOT
$30T
Generational transfer with no donor-grade planning layer.

DAFs, bequests, and bunching require a $400/hr financial planner — most donors never get one. An ad-hoc copilot puts planner-grade reasoning in every donor's pocket, on demand.

UNLOCK · planning copilot → planner-grade at zero marginal cost
THE THESIS None of these gaps are fixable with better forms or another rating site. Every one requires an agent that reasons over live, multi-source signal. These numbers become the platform's benchmarks: each AI surface tracks and optimizes against them, continuously learning to beat industry standards.

FOR ONE-TIME STRATEGIC GIFTS
Tax optimization strategies
Bunching, DAF timing, capital gains harvesting, QCD planning.
Legacy & bequest planning
Estate gifts, charitable trusts, multi-year pledge structures.
Grounded in donor context

HUMAN IN THE LOOP
This agent never executes. It models scenarios, surfaces trade-offs, and lets the donor decide. When ready, one-click checkout applies the plan.

§ 10   Development Roadmap

Five phases · ~15 months to full platform

Ship the MVP in 90 days, then compound.

Phase 01 · Q1
MVP + Profiling Agent

990 parser, ProPublica / Charity Navigator APIs, and the donor profiling agent that learns intent live as donors explore.

Phase 02 · Q2
LLM Charity Ingestion + User Testing

Web-grounded LLM pipeline profiles non-enrolled charities; campaign extraction with citation provenance. User experience testing, MVP refinement, and PMF assessment.

Phase 03 · Q3
Sentiment + Crowd Campaigns + Obat Onboarding

Reddit / X.com sentiment synthesis into Trust Index; AI scout for crowd-sourced fundraisers. OBAT Health integration begins with weekly feedback on Passport & Agentic features.

Phase 04 · Q4
Onboarding 3+ Non-Profits

Recruit and onboard 3 additional nonprofits beyond OBAT. Open nonprofit API for project & impact data sync; validate the B2B partnership model.

Phase 05 · Q5
Scale to 20 Non-Profits

Expand to 20 global health and development orgs. Monthly Giving Agent, Crisis Responder mode, and ad-hoc planning copilot (DAF / legacy / bunching) go live.

VALIDATION PARTNER
OBAT Health
OBAT commits to integrating the platform on launch, recruiting 3 additional charities within 6 months, and providing weekly feedback on the Passport & Agentic features — validating the sector-scale model before broader rollout.
